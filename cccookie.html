All cookies are airfryed and dehydrated to allow maximum storage life with no artificial flavours,colouring or perservatives.

Storage Instructions: Cookies should be stored in an airtight jar/container (such as a mason jar) or airtight bag (such as the original packaging) on the counter for up to 4 months. Alternatively they can be kept in the fridge for up to 6 months. Cookies will not harm your pup after this date however will be become quite hard & stale!
